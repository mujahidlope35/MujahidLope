About MSU–MCEST

A Legacy of Learning in the Heart of Mindanao

MSU–MCEST (Mindanao State University – Maigo College of Education, Science and Technology) is a premier state university campus located in Maigo, Lanao del Norte, Philippines.

As part of the MSU System, MCEST is dedicated to producing globally competitive graduates equipped with the knowledge, skills, and values needed to contribute meaningfully to national development and the upliftment of Mindanaoan communities.

The campus offers programs across education, science, technology, and business — all delivered with a commitment to academic rigor, community engagement, and sustainable development.

Unity in Diversity

At MSU-MCEST, we celebrate the harmony between Muslim and non-Muslim students, fostering mutual respect, understanding, and cooperation. Our campus is a place where diversity strengthens community, and everyone is valued regardless of culture or belief. Together, we build friendships, promote peace, and create a welcoming environment for all.
